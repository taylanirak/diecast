1.2.	INDIVIDUAL USERS

1.2.1.	Guest Users (unregistered)

Guest users can:

•	Browse all marketplace listings
•	Search vehicles with full search functionality
•	Use advanced filters (brand, scale, price, condition, etc.)
•	Sort results (newest, price, popularity)
•	View detailed vehicle information
•	View seller public profiles (limited info)
•	View featured collections (read-only)
•	View Digital Garages of paid members (read-only)
•	Read product reviews
•	Read seller ratings
•	View marketplace statistics
•	Access help center and FAQ
•	Add items to cart (session-based)
•	Calculate shipping costs
•	Complete purchases via guest checkout
•	Receive order confirmation email
•	Track orders via email link

Guest users cannot: 

•	Cannot create listings
•	Cannot save favorites/wishlists
•	Cannot save cart across sessions
•	Cannot participate in trades
•	Cannot rate or comment
•	Cannot message sellers directly
•	Cannot create collections or Digital Garage
•	Cart expires after 24 hours of inactivity
•	Limited access to seller contact information

To encourage guest users to sign up: 
•	After viewing 10 listings: "Create account to save favorites"
•	When trying to message seller: "Register to contact sellers"
•	After first purchase: "Create account to track orders easily"

# Downgrade Policy (Non-Payment):

•	**Grace Period (7 days after payment failure):**
•	All premium features remain active
•	Email reminders sent (day 1, 3, 5, 7)
•	SMS reminder (day 5)
•	In-app notification banner

Downgrade to Free (Day 8):
•	Account automatically downgraded to Free Member
•	Listings beyond free tier limit (5-10) → deactivated but saved
•	Digital Garage → set to private, view-only access maintained
•	Featured listings → removed from featured slots
•	Active trades → allowed to complete, no new trades
•	Ads → start appearing
•	Analytics → read-only, historical data preserved
•	Messages → limit to 50/day
•	Saved searches → reduced to 5
  
**What's Preserved:**
•	All historical data
•	Collections (accessible but not public)
•	Reviews and ratings
•	Order history
•	Favorites
•	Account settings

**Reactivation:**
•	Pay outstanding balance
•	All features restored immediately
•	Listings reactivated automatically
•	Digital Garage made public again
•	No data loss

Rating System for Individual Paid Members:

**Overall Rating (1-5 stars):**
Calculated from:
•	- Product accuracy (40%): Item as described
•	- Communication (20%): Response time and quality
•	- Shipping (20%): Speed and packaging
•	- Trade fairness (20%): For members who trade

**Reputation Scores:**
•	- **Rising Star**: 10-50 successful transactions, 4.5+ rating
•	- **Trusted Seller**: 50-200 transactions, 4.7+ rating
•	- **Elite Collector**: 200+ transactions, 4.8+ rating
•	- **Hall of Fame**: 500+ transactions, 4.9+ rating, 2+ years

**Special Recognitions:**
•	"Fast Shipper": 95%+ items shipped within 24 hours
•	"Fair Trader": 90%+ trade satisfaction
•	"Responsive": Average response time < 2 hours
•	"Collector Expert": Specialization in specific brand/category
•	"Community Champion": High engagement in forums/collections
